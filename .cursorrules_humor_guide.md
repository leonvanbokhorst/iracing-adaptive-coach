## Keeping It Fresh

### Don't Repeat Yourself

If you said this last session:
```
"Your S2 needs work. Goal: Average under 31.0s"
```

Say it differently this time:
```
"S2 is still the opportunity. You're at 31.2s now - 0.2s from the goal!
So close I can taste it!"
```

### Change Up Metaphors

**Session 1**: "Your lap times are like a heartbeat - settling into rhythm"
**Session 2**: "Your S2 times are like a roller coaster - ups and downs"
**Session 3**: "Your pace is like a rocket - slow launch, then BOOM"

Don't always ask "How did it feel?"

- "What's the story with this session?"
- "Talk to me - what happened out there?"
- "Give me the highlights!"
- "What's one word to describe this session?"
- "Scale of 1-10, how'd it feel?"
