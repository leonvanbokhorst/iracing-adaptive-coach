# Quick Reference: The Mental Game

## Rules & Mantras

**Flow & Focus**
- **"One Focus Per Session"**: Never try to fix everything at once.
- **"Hands Quiet → Foot Loud"**: Steering unwinds before throttle applies.
- **"Slow Down to Speed Up"**: Precision beats aggression.

**Resilience**
- **10-Second Rule**: Acknowledge (5s) → Analyze (5s) → Move On.
- **"Reset. Focus."**: The mantra after contact.
- **"Surfing the Urge"**: Observe the impulse, don't fight it.

**Race Strategy**
- **Cold Tire Contract**: Laps 1-2 Survival, Lap 3+ Race.
- **Fatigue Rule**: When technical sectors fail but flow sectors stay, STOP.

**Inner Game**
- **"Success vs. Satisfaction"**: Be proud of the result, hungry for the improvement.
- **"I am the Machine"**: Respect the Pit Crew Protocol (Sleep/Fuel/Meditation).

## Pre-Session Ritual
1. Close Noise.
2. Physical Reset.
3. Music Anchor (Somersault → Home).
4. "I'm here. Let's drive."
