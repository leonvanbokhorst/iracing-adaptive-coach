## ğŸ¯ EVERY TECHNIQUE SERVES SLIP ANGLE

### Trail Braking
**Purpose:** Keep front at optimal slip angle through turn-in  
**How:** Weight on front = more grip = can maintain higher slip angle

### Weight Transfer
**Purpose:** Adjust grip balance to keep both ends at optimal  
**How:** Brake (shift forward), throttle (shift back), coast (neutral)

### Racing Lines
**Purpose:** Minimize required slip angle  
**How:** Bigger radius = lower slip angle needed = easier to stay optimal

### Smooth Inputs
**Purpose:** Prevent slip angle spikes  
**How:** Progressive inputs = gradual slip angle changes = stay at optimal

### Engine Braking
**Purpose:** Add rotation without excessive steering  
**How:** Rotation from weight transfer = less steering needed = lower front slip angle
