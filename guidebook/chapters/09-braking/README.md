## Introduction: The Complete Braking System

Braking is the most misunderstood skill in racing. Most drivers think it's binary: "brake hard" or "trail brake." The reality? Every braking zone is a sophisticated three-phase process, and mastery of ALL three phases is what separates fast laps from REALLY fast laps.

**The Three Phases:**

1. **Brake Application** - How you GET to peak pressure (often overlooked!)
2. **Threshold Braking** - Maintaining peak pressure on a straight line
3. **Trail Braking** - Progressive release while turning

This chapter covers the complete braking system from theory to application, with specific focus on the Ray FF1600 and iRacing's unique physics characteristics.
